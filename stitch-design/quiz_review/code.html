Question 1
check_circle Correct

What is the primary effect of an increase in the central bank's discount rate on the broader economy?

A Increased consumer spending and business investment
B Reduced inflation and slower economic growth done
C Decline in the exchange rate of the national currency
info View Explanation expand_more
Increasing the discount rate makes borrowing more expensive for commercial banks, which in turn raises interest rates for consumers and businesses. This tightening of monetary policy typically leads to reduced spending, which cools down inflation but may slow overall economic growth.

cancel Incorrect

According to the Solow Growth Model, what is the primary driver of long-term sustainable growth in income per capita?

A High rates of physical capital accumulation Your Choice
B Exogenous technological progress check_circle
C Expansion of the labor force
lightbulb Explanation

While capital accumulation increases the level of income, it cannot sustain long-term growth due to diminishing returns to capital. In the basic Solow model, only continuous technological progress (exogenous) can explain long-run growth in output per worker.

Question 3
check_circle Correct

Based on the following Supply and Demand curve, identify the equilibrium price point.

A $45.00
B $60.00
info View Explanation expand_more
The equilibrium is reached at the intersection of the Supply and Demand curves. Following the horizontal axis from the intersection point leads to the price of $45.00.
